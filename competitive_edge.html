Competitive Edge

  • Focus on single company service
  • Require significant investment in app development
  • Limited regulatory compliance support
  • Compete for same driver pool

Ride Comparison Apps:

  • Only provide price comparison
  • Redirect to other apps for booking

  • Limited revenue model (ads or referrals)

  • Complete ecosystem connecting all parties
  • SaaS model with recurring revenue
  • Regulatory compliance built-in
  • Advanced analytics for all stakeholders
  • Multiple revenue streams (subscriptions, commissions, services)
